5. Project ideas

  • Overview

    • The project will be about creating a turn- based, strategy game that will have a gacha system. There will be a large amount of waifus, husbandos and equipment for the player to collect to be able to form up different team compositions to perform differently through-out the game and farm for upgrade materials that the game’s contents have to provide.

  • Motivation

    • This project is inspired by many turn-based and gacha games that I have played before: Granblue Fantasy, Bang dream, Honkai Impact 3, the Persona series, some Final Fantasy games, etc… There are different things that I have taken in interests from the mentioned games that always make their sales on towering positions such as: good character design; engaging, fun gameplay and combat; a wide variety of characters with different personalities; and good story telling. I want to create such an amazing gacha game that will gather many players who are interested in the genre not because I just want to make money, since developing video games is also what I am passionate about, especially turn-based gacha game.

  • Description

    • The complete product will be a role-playing video game with turn based combat, and gacha (capsule-toy vending machine) system. The player will start out with a team of the starter units in the game, as they venture through the story, they will gain different types of currency starting with the coin which will be used for upgrading, trading for materials in the shop, uncapping characters and equipment. Then, we have the crystal, which is the currency that will be used for pulling the gacha for chances of recruiting better characters that come along with a weapon, this indicates that pulling the gacha for just characters is clearly not enough, because the player will also need to acquire better weapon to advance further in the story as well as clearing the raids, or quests to farm materials for uncapping the characters and equipment, the materials can also be used for crafting new weapons, armor and accessories which can be accessed in the shop tab. Characters will have different weapons proficiency for example: This one character can only use weapons that are staff, dagger type whereas the other character can only use weapons that are melee, axe type. But all characters are still able to use any armor, accessories that the player has. Once the player have reached a certain point in the story, this is where the grind actually begins. Yes, they will have to farm for more experience to reach to the required rank level to attend certain raids in search for sources to gain new materials, and farm for new weapons. There will be events that come and go, so when an event is active, it is advised that the players should start farming these events for specific items so that they can trade for upgrades, the equipment that has the event’s theme, and the materials in the event shop. The player can also earn crystals from the events but there is a cap to it. As the player proceeds closer to the end of the story, they will unlock the most challenging raids, and quests for them to start farming for end-game materials, and chances for new weapons to drop from these end-game raids. Quests that are for materials farming can only be done by just the player and their party, whereas the raids will have limited amount of players that can join in and help each other out to finish the raid and attain the rewards (each player will get different drops because the drop rate varies). Aside from all the grinding for materials and equipment, the player can also build up relationship bonds between each character that they have recruited by sending gifts, the gifts can be obtained through buying in the shop tab and it will cost just coins, but they will be very high-priced. Once the player have reached the maximum level of a relationship with a character they will have the option to make that character call the player in any given nickname they want. By that time the player will be able to read through special dialogues from the maxed bond character on how he/she feels about the player. So the end-game activities for the players would be farming for coins to buy gifts and build up relationship bonds with all the characters they have, helping other players in any raid, stocking up resources for possible future updates.
